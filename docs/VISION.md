# Baseline Vision

Version: 1.0

---

## Mission

Create the easiest and friendliest way for tennis players to find games, build relationships and become better players.

People should open Baseline not because they need an app.

They should open it because they want to play tennis.

---

## Core Principles

Every feature must satisfy at least one of these principles:

- Help people play more often.
- Help people find better partners.
- Help people build trust.
- Help people improve.
- Help grow local tennis communities.

If a feature does none of these, it should not be built.

---

## Product Pillars

### 1. Find a Match

Players should find a game in under 30 seconds.

Future ideas:

- Smart recommendations
- Nearby matches
- Level matching
- Favourite courts

---

### 2. Trusted Community

Baseline should become a trusted tennis community.

Future ideas:

- Coach Verified Level
- Reliability Score
- Level Accuracy
- Report Abuse
- Verified Coaches

---

### 3. Tennis Passport

Every player builds a personal tennis history.

Future ideas:

- Match Timeline
- Courts Played
- Favourite Partners
- Total Matches
- Playing Streak
- Match History

---

### 4. Friends

Players should never lose contact after a great match.

Future ideas:

- QR Friend
- Add Friend
- Favourite Partners
- Invite Again
- Private Groups

**QR Friend Vision**

After a match, one player opens their QR code. The other player scans it. Friend request is created instantly.

No phone numbers. No Telegram usernames. No searching. Only one scan.

---

### 5. Player Growth

Future ideas:

- Statistics
- Rating History
- Tennis DNA
- Playing Style
- Progress Graph

---

### 6. Events

Future ideas:

- Tournaments
- Round Robin
- Knockout
- Club Events
- League Seasons

---

## Product Philosophy

Baseline is not just a booking application.

Baseline is a tennis community.

Success is not measured by the number of matches created.

Success is measured by how many lasting tennis friendships were created.

---

## Long-term Dream

A player can travel to any city.

Open Baseline.

Find a game.

Meet great people.

Keep them as friends.

Play together again.

Grow together.

That is the future of Baseline.

---

> **People come for matches. They stay for the community.**
