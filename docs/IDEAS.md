# Product Ideas

**Purpose:** a brainstorming parking lot for ideas intentionally out of
MVP scope. Nothing here is scheduled.

**What belongs here:** a short idea description, `Status: Future`, and a
priority. No implementation details, no architecture, no database design.

**What must never be duplicated here:** anything with a phase or
schedule (→ `docs/ROADMAP.md`, or `docs/BACKLOG.md` once it has a Goal
and MVP/Phase tier — if an idea graduates onto the backlog, move it,
don't keep both), an accepted decision (→ `docs/PRODUCT_DECISIONS.md`),
or a fleshed-out design.

---

## Community

### QR Friend

After a match, players can scan each other's QR code and instantly become friends inside Baseline.

**Status:** Future
**Priority:** High

---

### Favourite Partners

Allow players to save favourite partners and invite them again.

**Status:** Future
**Priority:** High

---

### Tennis Passport

Personal timeline of all matches. Includes date, match type, court, opponents and partners.

**Status:** Future
**Priority:** High

---

### Courts Collection

Track every court where a player has played.

**Status:** Future
**Priority:** Medium

---

### Reliability Score

Measure player reliability based on attendance, cancellations and completed matches. Does not use player ratings.

**Status:** Future
**Priority:** High

---

### Level Confidence

Measure how accurately a player's self-rated level matches community feedback. Coach verification remains a separate system.

**Status:** Future
**Priority:** Medium

---

### Play Again

After every match ask: "Would you play with this player again?" Used internally for recommendations. Answers are never exposed publicly.

**Status:** Future
**Priority:** High

---

### Tennis DNA

Generate personal tennis insights — favourite match type, favourite courts, most frequent partners, best level range.

**Status:** Future
**Priority:** Low
